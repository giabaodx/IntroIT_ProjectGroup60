SOME DEMONSTRATIONS OF OUR PRODUCT:



Visit our App Mall website.



The purpose of this project is to develop a website that can assist users in using software from a variety of operating systems. Our App Mall supports users' ability to purchase apps in installments and allows users to create an App Mall ID account. Our team's developed functionalities enable a large number of users to use apps that are restricted to specific operating systems. Additionally, we have a policy that gives students and office workers preference when it comes to monthly installment purchases. This policy enables them to purchase installments and use multiple applications concurrently without worrying about financial matters. Our team works to make customers' lives easier and more convenient. Additionally, all five members of The Five Horseman collective are currently enrolled in school, so we are aware of how challenging it can be to be constrained by financial constraints at a time when there are paid learning apps. The goal of The Five Horseman's project was to build a website that would make it simple for office workers and students to use programs from a variety of other operating systems. Early on in the group project, we were able to handle almost every issue while staying within the group's parameters. Instead of the current three categories, our team will continuously update more categories and add more applications for users in the later stages of the website.
